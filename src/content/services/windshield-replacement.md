A cracked or shattered windshield is more than an eyesore. It's a structural part of your car that holds up the roof and backs your airbags. When a chip is too far gone to fix, a proper **windshield replacement in Fort Myers** gets you back on the road safely, and we do the whole thing wherever your car is parked.

## Why replace instead of repair?

A [windshield repair](/windshield-repair) is the goal whenever it's safe. It's faster and usually cheaper. But once a crack runs longer than about three inches, reaches the edge of the glass, or sits in the driver's line of sight, the windshield loses strength and a replacement is the right call. Southwest Florida heat is hard on damaged glass. A chip that looked minor in the morning can spider across the whole windshield by afternoon, especially after a blast of A/C on a hot day. When in doubt, send us a photo and we'll tell you straight.
