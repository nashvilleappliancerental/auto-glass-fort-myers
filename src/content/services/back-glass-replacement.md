A shattered rear window leaves your car exposed and your trunk full of glass pebbles. **Back glass replacement in Fort Myers** gets your vehicle sealed up again. And because the rear window is tempered glass, the cleanup matters as much as the install. We handle both.

## Back glass isn't like your windshield

Your windshield is laminated, so it cracks and stays in one piece. Your rear window is tempered, so when it breaks it shatters into thousands of small pebbles across your trunk, seats, and carpet. That's safer in a collision, but it means a proper job includes a full vacuum-out, not just dropping in new glass. Most back windows also carry a defroster grid, and some hold the car's radio antenna, both of which we reconnect.
