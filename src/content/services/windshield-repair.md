## Why repair beats waiting

Glass damage spreads, and Southwest Florida is about the worst place for it. Daily heat, strong sun, and the temperature swing when you blast the A/C all stress a chip until it runs into a long crack. Love-bug season and gravel kicked up on US-41 don't help. Catch it while it's small and a quick resin repair restores the strength and clarity. Wait too long and it crosses into [windshield replacement](/windshield-replacement) territory.
